## ROSS CAMERON'S EXACT WATCHLIST PROCESS

### NIGHTLY (7-8 PM) — After Hours Scan
- Pull up phone, check **after hours top gainers scanner**
- Look for: big moves after hours — but note: "if you have a really big move after hours,
  by pre-market you've kind of missed the party" (e.g. PLRZ example)
- These become candidates for next morning's pre-market check

### MORNING (6-7 AM) — Pre-Market Scan
- Only thing Ross looks at at 6am: **pre-market scanner**
- Top gainers / top losers pre-market
- Stocks that ran after hours often fade at open — wait for confirmation

### FILTERS APPLIED (in order)
1. **Percentage gain** — how much it moved
2. **Price** — what price level
3. **Float** — low float = more explosive
4. **Relative Volume** — how much volume vs. average
5. **Total volume** — absolute volume level
6. **Short interest** — check later (covered in Ch3 Part 4)
7. **Short sale restriction (SSR)** — covered in Ch3 Part 4

### ENTRY SIGNAL REFERENCES
- **First pullback** — covered in depth in Chapter 5 (today/chapter 5 chart patterns)
- **First candle to make a new high** — key entry confirmation
- **New highs** — Ross pushes back on naive new-high interpretations:
  "new highs the company said no no you're not because we're selling"

### RISK PROCESS
- **Max loss determined BEFORE entry** — not during
- "when we get in, our max loss is determined. we're not exposing ourselves to unlimited risk"

### WATCHLIST SIZE
- Narrow down to **5-10 stocks max** before market open
- Focus on quality over quantity

### SCANNER TYPES USED
- After hours top gainers (nightly)
- Pre-market top gainers (morning)
- Relative volume scanner
- Gap up scanner
