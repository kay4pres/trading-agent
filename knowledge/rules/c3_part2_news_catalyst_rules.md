### Stock Selection (25 mentions)
- Relative Volume: look for stocks with RV 5x+ above average

- Gap and go: trade at the open after catalyst confirmation
- Partial gap vs. full gap — partial gaps are more tradeable
- Opening range consolidation before continuation

### Risk Management (159 mentions — dominant topic)
- Max loss is defined BEFORE entering the trade
- Stop loss placement is based on ATR or recent volatility
- 1:2 risk/reward minimum
- Position sizing: risk no more than 1-2% of account per trade

### Watchlist Building
- Pre-market scanner for gap-ups
- After-hours catalyst check
- Consolidate watchlist to 5-10 stocks max before market open
